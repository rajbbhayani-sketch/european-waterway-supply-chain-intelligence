# European Waterway Supply Chain Intelligence Platform

## Overview

This project is a multi-page Power BI analytics platform focused on European inland waterway logistics and supply chain intelligence.

The dashboard provides operational, sustainability, congestion, and predictive analytics across major European freight corridors and ports.

---

## Business Objectives

* Monitor cargo movement across European logistics corridors
* Track operational delays and delivery reliability
* Analyze sustainability and CO2 emissions
* Evaluate port congestion and turnaround efficiency
* Support predictive logistics and operational risk monitoring

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Control Tower

Executive-level KPIs and operational overview.

### 2. Corridor Operations Intelligence

Freight flow, corridor performance, and delay hotspot analysis.

### 3. Sustainability & Emissions Intelligence

CO2 reduction, emissions tracking, and green logistics analytics.

### 4. Port Operations & Congestion Analytics

Port efficiency, waiting times, congestion monitoring, and throughput analysis.

### 5. Predictive & AI Analytics

Forecast cargo demand, operational risk analysis, and predictive trend monitoring.
